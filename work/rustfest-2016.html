RustFest 2016

Client Honeypot Involvement UX/UI

Europe's first conference dedicated to the Rust programming language. Rust is a general purpose programming language sponsored by Mozilla Research. It is designed to be a "safe, concurrent, practical language", supporting functional and imperative-procedural paradigms. The first conference took place in Berlin, our second in Kyiv and the third edition of RustFest will take place as a two-day event in Zurich, Switzerland.
